Pianist Jia Meng is an emerging artist from Dalian, China. In addition to performing at several venues throughout China and United States, she also enjoys sharing her music with the local community, performing at hospitals and retirement homes such as Northwestern Woman's Hospital,The Breakers, Music@Grace, and many Churches. She have successfully performed solo recital and chamber performance in Chicago area. She also has appeared on the front cover pianist of the Clavier Companion magazine (July/Aug 2017 issue).

Meng get her Bachelor degree from Chicago College of Performing Arts, and she has been on the faculty at Xilin Arts Academy for the past four years. She has played in master classes with many famous artists, including Meng-Chieh Liu, Adam Neiman, Idil Biret, Edward Auer, Tamás Ungár, Jonathan Bass,and Yael Weiss. Previously she also studied at the middle and high school attached to Shenyang Conservatory.
